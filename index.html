üî¨ Methodology

Selection Criteria: To manage costs effectively, we focus our analysis on Chief Investigators with 3 or more Discovery Projects (DPs), representing the most active researchers in the Australian academic system.

Gender Identification: We use OpenAI's search-enabled models (gpt-4o-mini-search-preview) to perform web searches for each researcher and analyze names, pronouns, and publicly available information to determine gender identity.

Research Areas: Extracted from web search results including academic profiles, publications, and institutional pages.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Disclaimer

We sincerely apologize for any gender misidentification. Gender identity is personal and complex, and our automated analysis may not always be accurate. We recognize that:

  • Gender cannot always be determined from names or public information
  • Some individuals may use different pronouns or identify differently than our analysis suggests
  • Non-binary and other gender identities may not be properly represented in our current binary classification
